About & History 📘

Kapenguria School (formerly Kapenguria Boys High School) is a public boys’ boarding secondary school in West Pokot County, Kenya. The institution began as a primary school in 1938 and later became a secondary school in 1984. It has grown into one of the leading schools in the region.

The school is located near Kapenguria town, the administrative headquarters of West Pokot County, along the A1 road northeast of Kitale. Students live on campus throughout the school year.

Academics 📚

The school offers sciences, mathematics, languages, and technical subjects that prepare students for careers and university education.
