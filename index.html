Kapenguria School 🎓

Motto: Hardwork Pays 💪🔥

Kapenguria School is the best school located in West Pokot County 🌍. The school is known for academic excellence 📚, discipline 🏫, and outstanding performance.

With more than 80 professional teachers 👨‍🏫👩‍🏫, students are guided to excel and achieve success 🌟.

Principal: MOSES NDEDA 👔

Academics 📚

The school offers a wide range of subjects including sciences 🔬, mathematics ➗, languages 🗣️, and technical subjects. Students are prepared for both academic and real-life success 🚀.

The school also leads in co-curricular activities ⚽🎭🎵 including sports, drama, music, and science congress.

School Values 💡

Excellence 🏆

Excellence means striving to be the best ⭐. Students are encouraged to work hard 💪 and achieve top academic results while maintaining discipline and focus.

Integrity 🤝

Integrity means honesty and responsibility 👍. Students are taught to always do the right thing, respect others, and build strong moral character.

Teamwork 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦

Teamwork means working together 🤝. Students learn cooperation and unity through group work, sports, and shared responsibilities.
